Understanding Suicide Risks

Suicide is a serious public health issue that affects individuals and families across all communities. Understanding the risks and signs can help us provide support to those in need.

Signs to Look For

  • Talking about wanting to die or to kill themselves
  • Feeling hopeless or having no reason to live
  • Feeling trapped or in unbearable pain
  • Being a burden to others
  • Withdrawing from friends, family, or society

How to Help

If you or someone you know is struggling, please reach out for help. Here are some resources:

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 1-800-273-TALK (1-800-273-8255)
  • Crisis Text Line: Text "HELLO" to 741741
  • Emergency Services: Dial 911

Support is Available

It's essential to understand that help is available, and you are not alone. Reach out to a trusted friend, family member, or professional for support.
